## Why the fleet won (the real pattern)
Every fleet win came from **one lens catching a concrete, cited defect the single pass missed** —
not better prose:
- Task 1: adversary lens caught a **math error in prism.md's own rationale** (majority-of-3 means
  the lone Sonnet canNOT be decisive when *both* Opus miss — the doc claims it can).
- Task 2: panel surfaced **breadth** (undefined-Jaccard on anchorless tasks; size-normalization;
  weight collinearity) the single pass's one deep insight didn't cover.
- Task 4: safety lens found **`prism-guard.sh:32` does not block a local `git commit`** — so Isolate
  is the sole backstop against committing to main.
These are **real bugs in Prism itself**, surfaced as a by-product. The single pass was excellent on
Task 3 (genuine tie) — the fleet's edge is grounded defect-finding, not general quality.
